# JLPT N5 Adverbs

---
## 今日

**Reading:** きょう
**Meaning:** today
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #adverb, #time, #common

### Example Sentences
- 今日は暑いです - It's hot today
- 今日何をしますか - What will you do today?
- 今日は休みです - Today is a day off

### Notes
Time adverb. Does not require particles when used as time expression. Very commonly used in daily conversation.
