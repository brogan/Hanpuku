# JLPT N5 Vocabulary

---
## 食べる

**Reading:** たべる
**Meaning:** to eat
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #verb, #common

### Example Sentences
- 朝ご飯を食べる - To eat breakfast
- 寿司を食べたい - I want to eat sushi
- 何を食べますか - What will you eat?

### Notes
Ichidan verb (ru-verb). Very common daily verb.

## こんにちは

**Reading:** こんにちは
**Meaning:** hello, good afternoon
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #greeting, #common

### Example Sentences
- こんにちは、元気ですか - Hello, how are you?
- 先生、こんにちは - Hello, teacher

### Notes
Most common greeting for daytime. Literally means "as for today..."

## ありがとう

**Reading:** ありがとう
**Meaning:** thank you
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #common, #polite

### Example Sentences
- ありがとうございます - Thank you (polite)
- どうもありがとう - Thank you very much

### Notes
Add ございます for more politeness. Essential phrase for daily life.

## 学校

**Reading:** がっこう
**Meaning:** school
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #noun, #common

### Example Sentences
- 学校に行く - To go to school
- 学校は楽しいです - School is fun
- どの学校ですか - Which school?

### Notes
Compound of 学 (learning) + 校 (school building).
