# JLPT N5 Nouns - People, Family & Society
#
# ABOUT THIS SET:
# This set contains essential N5-level nouns related to people, family relationships,
# occupations, social relationships, and general people-related vocabulary.
# These are foundational words for talking about yourself, your family, and others.
#
# CATEGORIES COVERED:
# - Family members (immediate and extended)
# - Occupations and professions
# - People descriptors (age, gender, etc.)
# - Social relationships and titles
# - General people-related words
#
# TOTAL ITEMS: ~110 nouns
#
# Note: Many family terms have both humble (talking about your own family) and
# honorific (talking about others' families) forms. These distinctions are noted.

---
## 人

**Reading:** ひと
**Meaning:** person, people
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #noun, #people, #common

### Example Sentences
- あの人は誰ですか - Who is that person?
- この町には人が多いです - There are many people in this town
- いい人です - They're a good person

### Notes
Most basic word for "person/people." Counter: ~人 (ひとり、ふたり、さんにん, etc.). Can be prefix in compounds: 人々 (ひとびと - people). Very common in daily conversation.

## 方

**Reading:** かた
**Meaning:** person (polite)
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #noun, #people, #polite
