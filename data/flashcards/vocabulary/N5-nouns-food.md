# Common Food Vocabulary

---
## ご飯

**Reading:** ごはん
**Meaning:** rice, meal
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #food, #common, #essential

### Example Sentences
- ご飯を食べる - To eat rice/a meal
- 朝ご飯（あさごはん） - Breakfast
- 昼ご飯（ひるごはん） - Lunch
- 晩ご飯（ばんごはん） - Dinner

### Notes
Essential word meaning both cooked rice and meal in general. Very common in daily conversation.
