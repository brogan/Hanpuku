# JLPT N5 Verbs

---
## 食べる

**Reading:** たべる
**Meaning:** to eat
**Type:** vocabulary
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #verb, #ichidan, #common

### Example Sentences
- 朝ご飯を食べる - To eat breakfast
- 寿司を食べたい - I want to eat sushi
- 何を食べますか - What will you eat?

### Notes
Ichidan verb (ru-verb). Conjugates as: 食べます (polite), 食べた (past), 食べない (negative), 食べて (te-form).
