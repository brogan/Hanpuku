# Japanese Greetings and Common Phrases

---
## おはようございます

**Reading:** おはようございます
**Meaning:** good morning
**Type:** phrase
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #greeting, #polite

### Example Sentences
- おはようございます、先生 - Good morning, teacher
- おはよう (casual form) - Morning (to friends/family)

### Notes
Used until around 10-11 AM. Drop ございます for casual situations.

---

## いただきます

**Reading:** いただきます
**Meaning:** expression before eating (lit: I humbly receive)
**Type:** phrase
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #common, #culture

### Example Sentences
- (Before eating) いただきます
- 食べる前にいただきますと言う - Say "itadakimasu" before eating

### Notes
Essential cultural phrase said before every meal. Shows gratitude for the food.

---

## ごちそうさまでした

**Reading:** ごちそうさまでした
**Meaning:** expression after eating (thank you for the meal)
**Type:** phrase
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #common, #culture

### Example Sentences
- (After eating) ごちそうさまでした
- 美味しかったです。ごちそうさまでした - It was delicious. Thank you for the meal.

### Notes
Said after finishing a meal. Shows appreciation to the cook and the food.

---

## すみません

**Reading:** すみません
**Meaning:** excuse me, sorry, thank you
**Type:** phrase
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #common, #polite

### Example Sentences
- すみません、トイレはどこですか - Excuse me, where is the toilet?
- すみません、間違えました - Sorry, I made a mistake
- (After receiving help) すみません - Thank you

### Notes
Versatile phrase used for apologizing, getting attention, or thanking. Very useful!

---

## おやすみなさい

**Reading:** おやすみなさい
**Meaning:** good night
**Type:** phrase
**Level:** N5
**Tags:** #jlpt-n5, #greeting

### Example Sentences
- おやすみなさい、また明日 - Good night, see you tomorrow
- おやすみ (casual) - Night

### Notes
Used when going to bed or saying goodbye late at night.
